# Privacy Policy

**App:** Cozypop Trails — Candy Trail Puzzle
**Effective date:** June 2, 2026
**Last updated:** July 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Omnigame** (“we”, “us”, “our”), the developer of **Cozypop Trails** (the “App”), handles information when you use the App. We do **not** require an account, and we do **not** ask for your name, email, phone number, or precise location. The App may show third-party ads and uses analytics, crash reporting, and advertising attribution to operate, improve, and measure the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

## 2. Scope

This Policy applies to the Cozypop Trails mobile application on Apple iOS. It does not apply to third-party websites or platforms, such as the Apple App Store, that have their own privacy policies.

## 3. Information We Collect and How We Use It

### 3.1 Information you provide to us
None. Cozypop Trails has no sign‑up, login, or account, and does not include any forms, messaging, or user‑generated content. We do not knowingly receive any directly identifying personal information from you.

### 3.2 Game progress and settings (stored only on your device)
Your level progress, current level, lives/“hearts” status, daily‑challenge completion and streak, and sound/haptics preferences are stored **locally on your device** (in standard iOS preferences storage). This gameplay state is used to operate the App and is removed when you delete the App.

### 3.3 Analytics, crash reports, and diagnostics
The App uses Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to understand gameplay interactions, performance, stability, crashes, and diagnostics. These services may process device identifiers, app instance identifiers, coarse device information, product interaction data, performance data, and crash data. We use this information to operate, debug, and improve the App.

### 3.4 Identifiers and tracking
The App may request App Tracking Transparency permission. If you allow tracking, the App and our advertising/attribution partners may use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and related event data to measure ad performance and attribution across apps and websites. If you do not allow tracking, we do not access the IDFA and use non-IDFA measurement such as IDFV, app instance identifiers, SKAdNetwork, and aggregated reporting where available. The App does not respond to browser “Do Not Track” (DNT) signals; no uniform standard for DNT has been adopted.

### 3.5 Advertising and purchases
The App may show third-party advertising, including interstitial ads and rewarded video ads. Rewarded ads are optional and can provide in-game benefits such as hints or heart refills. The App currently contains **no in‑app purchases**.

### 3.6 How we use the information
We use locally stored information only to:
- operate and maintain the App;
- remember your progress, preferences, lives/hearts status, and daily challenge state;
- provide the gameplay experience on your device.

We do **not** use this information to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you, and we do **not** sell it.

## 4. Third‑Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services: Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, AppLovin MAX and mediated ad networks, AppsFlyer, Facebook SDK, and Apple services such as the App Store and SKAdNetwork. These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 5. How We May Disclose Your Information

We do **not** sell your information for money. We may share limited identifiers, product interaction data, ad impression data, and diagnostic data with analytics, advertising, attribution, and crash-reporting providers to operate the App, show ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and improve reliability. We may also disclose information:
- to comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable governmental requests;
- to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, the public, or us;
- in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case we will require the recipient to honor this Policy.

## 6. International Data Transfer

The services we use may process information on servers in countries or regions outside your own. If you contact us by email for support, your message may also be processed by your email provider and ours according to their own terms and policies.

## 7. Your Choices and Opt‑Out

You control the information stored by the App:

- **Delete the App:** removing Cozypop Trails deletes locally stored progress and settings from your device.
- **Device controls:** you can manage device-level settings such as App Tracking Transparency, notifications, network access, and app deletion through iOS Settings.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information.

- **EEA/UK (GDPR):** rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. Because the App does not require an account and most App data is processed through device-level or app-level identifiers, we may be unable to associate App-collected data with a specific individual unless you provide enough information for us or our service providers to identify the relevant records. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority — in the EEA, your national data protection authority; in the UK, the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO); in Switzerland, the FDPIC.
- **California (CCPA/CPRA):** rights to know, delete, and correct, and to opt out of “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell your information for money. Some advertising attribution or measurement may be considered “sharing” under California law; you can limit tracking through iOS privacy controls and contact us with requests.

Because we do not collect directly identifying information or maintain accounts, we may be unable to associate App data with a specific individual; in some cases this may limit our ability to act on a request. To exercise any right, contact us using the details in Section 12.

## 9. Data Retention

Locally stored progress and settings remain on your device until you delete the App. Analytics, attribution, advertising, and crash data are retained by us and our service providers only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and service operation.

## 10. Security

We design the App to keep gameplay progress and settings on your device. However, no method of storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Children’s Privacy

Cozypop Trails is a general‑audience puzzle game and is **not directed to children under 13** (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.
