## Common Topics

### Gameplay and progress

Cozypop Trails stores level progress, hearts, daily challenge completion, streaks, and settings locally on your device. Deleting the App may remove this local progress.

### Hearts

Hearts are a local gameplay limiter. They regenerate automatically over time and cannot be purchased with real money.

### Ads and rewards

The App may show interstitial ads and optional rewarded video ads. Rewarded ads can provide local in-game benefits such as hints or heart refills after the ad is completed and the ad network confirms the reward. If no ad is available, please check your network connection and try again later. If you watched a rewarded ad but did not receive the reward, email us with your app version, device model, iOS version, the approximate time, and whether the placement was a hint, continue, or heart refill.

### Privacy and diagnostics

Progress, hearts, daily challenge status, and settings are stored locally on your device. The App may use analytics, crash reporting, advertising, attribution, and related service providers to operate, measure, improve, and monetize the App. For details, please review the Privacy Policy.
