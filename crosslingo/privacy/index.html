CrossLingo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026 · Version 1.0

CrossLingo ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a translation app for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use text, voice, and camera translation features.

1. Summary

CrossLingo does not require an account and does not operate a server that receives your typed text, microphone audio, speech transcripts, translations, photos, or conversation history.

Version 1.0 does not include ads, external purchase flows, or third-party analytics/attribution SDKs in the submitted app binary.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share the following information through our own services:

  • Voice recordings or live microphone audio
  • Speech transcripts, translations, or conversation history
  • Photos or images captured through the camera
  • Text you type into the App
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics events

3. Apple System Services

The App uses Apple-provided frameworks to perform its core functions. Depending on your device, iOS version, language, and system settings, Apple speech recognition and translation services may process requests on device or through Apple-operated services. We do not receive or store the content processed by Apple frameworks.

FeatureApple Framework or ServicePurpose
Voice translationAVFoundation and SpeechCapture speech from the microphone and convert speech to text.
Text and voice translationTranslationTranslate text between selected languages.
Camera translationAVFoundation, PhotosUI, and VisionCapture or select an image, recognize text, and translate it.

Apple's handling of data is governed by Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

4. Permissions the App Requests

PermissionWhy It Is Needed
MicrophoneRequired to capture speech for voice translation.
Speech RecognitionRequired to convert spoken words into text using Apple's Speech framework.
CameraRequired to capture photos of text for camera translation.
Photo Library SelectionUsed only when you choose an image from the system photo picker.

5. Local Storage

The App may store basic settings and local app state on your device using iOS app storage. If conversation history is created by the App, it is stored inside the App's local sandbox. Deleting the App removes its local app container subject to iOS behavior.

6. Purchases and Subscriptions

Version 1.0 does not present paid plans or external purchase options. If paid features are introduced in a future version, they will be offered through Apple's In-App Purchase system and this policy will be updated as needed.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is designed to function without an account or our own data collection backend. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will be revised when material changes are made.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing or the support email associated with the App.
