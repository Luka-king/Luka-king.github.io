CrossLingo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2026 · Version 1.3

CrossLingo ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a translation app for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use text, voice, camera, image translation, and CrossLingo Plus subscription features.

1. Summary

CrossLingo does not require you to create an account. We do not operate a server that receives your typed text, microphone audio, speech transcripts, translations, photos, or conversation history for translation processing.

CrossLingo Plus subscriptions are provided through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Our servers are used to load subscription catalog information, create purchase orders, verify Apple purchase results, and confirm membership status.

Marketing and attribution reporting are disabled in the App Review build. If enabled in a later marketing build, the App will update this policy and any required privacy disclosures before submission.

2. Translation Content We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share the following information through our own services:

  • Voice recordings or live microphone audio
  • Speech transcripts, translations, or conversation history
  • Photos or images captured through the camera
  • Text you type into the App

Depending on your device, iOS version, language, and system settings, Apple speech recognition and translation services may process requests on device or through Apple-operated services. We do not receive or store the content processed by Apple frameworks.

6. Local Storage

The App may store basic settings and local app state on your device using iOS app storage. This includes onboarding state, selected languages, subscription catalog cache, daily free translation count, and local conversation history if created by the App. Deleting the App removes its local app container subject to iOS behavior.

7. Purchases and Subscriptions

CrossLingo Plus is sold through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Apple processes payment information and manages billing, renewal, cancellation, and refunds. We do not receive your full payment card details.

The App may contact our subscription backend before and after an Apple purchase to create an order, report the Apple transaction, confirm membership, restore active subscriptions, and unlock paid features on this device.

8. Attribution and Analytics

The current App Review build disables marketing and attribution reporting. If a later build enables attribution or analytics, it may use services such as AppsFlyer, Meta/Facebook SDK, and our backend reporting endpoint, subject to App Store privacy requirements and any required system permission prompts.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data can be removed by deleting the App from your device. Subscription and purchase verification records may be retained as needed to provide the subscription, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and support customer service.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is designed to function without an account or our own data collection backend. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will be revised when material changes are made.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the CrossLingo Support page: https://luka-king.github.io/crosslingo/support/.
