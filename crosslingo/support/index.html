CrossLingo Support

Last Updated: June 10, 2026 · Version 1.1

CrossLingo is a translation app for text, voice, camera, and image translation on iPhone. This page provides support information for Version 1.0 and CrossLingo Plus.

Common Help Topics

  • Voice translation: Make sure Microphone and Speech Recognition permissions are enabled for CrossLingo in the iOS Settings app.
  • Siri and Dictation message: If iOS reports that Siri and Dictation are disabled, enable Dictation and Siri in system settings and check Screen Time restrictions.
  • Translation availability: Some language pairs may require Apple translation support or language downloads on the device. Try another language pair or follow the in-app download prompt.
  • Camera translation: Make sure Camera access is enabled. Photo Library access is only needed when you choose an image from the system photo picker.
  • CrossLingo Plus: Subscriptions are purchased through Apple In-App Purchase. Use Restore in the subscription screen if you already subscribed with the same Apple ID.
  • Canceling a subscription: Manage or cancel CrossLingo Plus from your Apple ID subscription settings. Deleting the App does not cancel an active subscription.

Contact Support

For CrossLingo support requests, open a support issue here:

https://github.com/Luka-king/Luka-king.github.io/issues/new?title=CrossLingo%20Support%20Request

Please include your device model, iOS version, CrossLingo version, selected languages, and steps to reproduce the issue. Do not include sensitive personal information, private conversations, or confidential translation content in a public issue.
